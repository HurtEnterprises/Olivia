STEP 1:

UNDERSTANDING YOUR LIFESTYLE

Instead of requiring you to fill out tons of paper at the doctor's office,I will walk you through an easy 10-minute survey that will be sent to the doctor of your choice!

 

All of the questions are from a 1-5 scale with "1" being "never / almost never" and 5 being "always / almost always." Please type in the blank which number best applies. The explanations of the number system will be on every survey page.

 

The questions are based on national health guidelines, and will be simple, similar to something like this:

"On a scale from 1-5, please indicate how often you go on walks."

 

Please answer these questions as honestly as possible, as that is the only way we can have the best, customized health visit for you!

STEP 2:

SCHEDULING YOUR VISIT

After you complete the quick and easy survey, I will show you the available dates and times of doctors in your area! Once you select the time, I will take care of coordinating with the doctor's office so that you do not have to do anything else but arrive at the appointment!

STEP 3:

ARRANGING YOUR TRANSPORT

The last step is indicating whether or not you need transportation assistance! If so, we will follow up with you closer to the appointment date with the necessary details.
